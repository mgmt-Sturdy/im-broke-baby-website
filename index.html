About

Jordan Green

Jordan Green is an award-winning financial influencer, content creator, and entrepreneur building platforms at the intersection of money, culture, and youth entertainment.

In 2022, he was named Highly Commended Online Financial Influencer of the Year by the British Bank Award.

Through Sturdy Off, a company co-founded by Jordan, he has fused groundbreaking dance and youth culture into brandable, high-impact content partnerships with global brands including Nike, Adidas, JD Sports, Wingstop, EMI, Universal Music Group, EMPIRE, and Honour. In just four years, the platform generated over 1 billion views and built a community of more than 3 million followers.

Jordan is currently building Dance Clash, a digital-first platform designed to showcase talented dancers across the UK and give them maximum visibility and exposure.
